Welcome to Noistroids! Here are the controls:

Turn left: Left arrow or the letter S

Turn right: Right arrow or the letter F

Shoot: Z or the letter J

Laser powerup: A or the letter U

If you reach zero energy, your ship will crash. Recharge by hovering near batteries.

Obtain points by shooting stuff shooting at you. No points for shooting other players.
